Chagar Hutang Resource Management System (CHARMS) Privacy Policy

CHARMS is a mobile and web-based application system designed to automate operations and management at Chagar Hutang Turtle Sanctuary, conducted by Conservation Management Solutions (CMS) and Sea Turtle Research Unit (SEATRU), as part of the digital transformation of sea turtle conservation efforts.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile and web application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Types of Data Collected

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about your privacy at CHARMS

How do I delete my account and data?

+

To permanently delete your CHARMS account:

  1. Open the CHARMS app
  2. Go to Settings > Account > Privacy & Security
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Read the warning and confirm by entering your password
  5. Tap "Permanently Delete My Account"

What happens after deletion:

  • Your account is immediately deactivated
  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Backup systems are purged within 90 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email

Data that may be retained:

  • Anonymized conservation data for research purposes
  • Log files for security purposes (12 months)
  • Data required for legal compliance

Note: Account deletion is irreversible. Make sure to download your data before deletion if you want to keep a copy.

How can I download my data?

+

To request a copy of your data:

  1. Go to Settings > Account > Privacy & Security
  2. Tap "Download My Data"
  3. Select the data categories you want:
    • Profile information
    • Conservation records and observations
    • Photos and media files
    • Usage history
    • All data (complete export)
  4. Choose your preferred format (CSV, JSON, PDF)
  5. Tap "Request Data Export"

Processing time:

  • Small exports (< 100MB): Within 1 hour
  • Medium exports (100MB - 1GB): Within 24 hours
  • Large exports (> 1GB): Up to 48 hours

Receiving your data:

  • You'll receive an email notification when ready
  • Download link is valid for 7 days
  • Files are provided in standard, portable formats
  • All files are password-protected for security

What we DON'T do:

  • Track your location when the app is not in use

How to manage location permissions:

On Android:

  1. Go to Settings > Apps > CHARMS > Permissions
  2. Tap "Location"

In the CHARMS app:

  • Settings > Privacy > Location Services
